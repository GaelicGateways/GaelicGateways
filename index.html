Welcome to Gaelic Gateways

Gaelic Gateways is a professional travel company providing private transport and guided tours for visitors across Ireland. Our experienced drivers are ATGI-Certified and Fáilte Ireland-Accredited, delivering safe, knowledgeable, and friendly journeys — from single transfers to multi-day bespoke tours.

Private Transfers

Airport pickups, hotel transfers, and inter-city journeys — punctual, comfortable vehicles.

Guided Day Tours

Expert-guided itineraries to iconic sights: Cliffs of Moher, Ring of Kerry, Giant's Causeway, and more.

Bespoke Multi-Day Tours

Custom routes, local experiences, and curated stays — we plan your perfect Irish getaway.
